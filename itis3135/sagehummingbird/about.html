About Our Business

Sage Hummingbird is a whimsical furniture shop where nature and comfort collide. Our collection includes couches and chairs that look like trees, logs, waterfalls, bushes, and even hummingbird feathers.

We believe your living space should feel alive and magical—just like a rainforest. Each piece brings the outdoors inside, creating a cozy jungle sanctuary.
