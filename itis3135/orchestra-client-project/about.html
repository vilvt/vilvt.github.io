Our Story

The UNC Charlotte Orchestra brings together students, faculty, and community members to celebrate the beauty of orchestral music. Each semester, the ensemble performs a wide range of works, from classical masterworks to contemporary compositions.

Meet the Conductor

Dr. Alan Yamamoto – Visiting Director of Orchestras, Department of Music, University of North Carolina at Charlotte.

Dr. Yamamoto leads the orchestra program at UNC Charlotte. From 2011–22 he conducted the Central Piedmont Opera Theater Orchestra and served on faculty for nine years. In 2021 he founded the Queens Chamber Orchestra at Queens University of Charlotte. His earlier work includes serving as Resident Conductor of the Charlotte Symphony Orchestra (2003–09) and the Colorado Music Festival (1994–03).

A champion of contemporary music, Dr. Yamamoto created the Modern Music Festival in Boulder and has conducted premieres of works by John Adams, Jennifer Higdon, and Chen Yi. He holds degrees from the University of Michigan, the University of California, Berkeley, and the University of Colorado Boulder. View full profile

Concertmaster — Fall 2025

Jacob Bell – Concertmaster, UNC Charlotte Orchestra

Jacob is a 20-year-old junior at UNC Charlotte, double-majoring in Music (BA track) and Computer Science (Software Engineering concentration). He is actively involved with the University Honors Program (UHP) and has been recognized in All-State Honors Orchestra (2021–2023).

His musical experience includes:

  • Winston-Salem Youth Orchestra (2020–2023)
  • International Lyric Academy Summer 2023
  • Opera Carolina Sit-in Violinist Summer 2023
  • UNC Charlotte Orchestra (2023–present)
  • Concertmaster, UNC Charlotte Orchestra (2025–present)
  • UNC Charlotte String Quartet (2025–present)
