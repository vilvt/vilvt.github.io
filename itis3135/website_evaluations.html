Website Evaluations

Won J You Studios

Won J You Studios is a design coaching & mentorship site with a clean and modern aesthetic, showcasing strong visuals, clear navigation, and well organized content.

  • Contrast: This site uses bold headings, vivid images, and clear distinction between call-to-action items and body text, which helps focus user attention. The website is bold and different from other websites visually.
  • Repetition: Font and heading styles are consistent, button/link styles are reused, layout patterns repeat in different sections, providing visual harmony.
  • Alignment: Images and text boxes are aligned to a grid, margins and spacing are consistent, navigation and content are placed in predictable positions.
  • Proximity: Related elements (like contact, services, navigation) are grouped, whitespace separates different content areas, making the page easier to scan and digest.

Arngren.net

Arngren.net is extremely cluttered, with many images, lots of links, visually chaotic layout, making it hard to find any hierarchy or know where to look first.

  • Contrast: Lacks clear contrast between important and less important elements; many items have similar visual weight; clickable items are not clearly distinguished.
  • Repetition: Many differing fonts, styles, image types; little consistency in how links/buttons/features are styled.
  • Alignment: Very irregular layout; elements appear randomly placed without alignment; text and images are not aligned on visible grid.
  • Proximity: Related content is not grouped; there is minimal whitespace; the site feels overwhelming because so much is crammed together.
